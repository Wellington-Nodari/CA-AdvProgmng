Welcome to
THE GILLIAN MOORE | B&B

The Gillian Moore is a family run Bed & Breakfast located in the village of Oranmore in Galway, Ireland on the edge of the beautiful East Coast.
We are only 200 metres from a sandy beach at Roscan Point and a twenty minute walk to Ballyloughane National Park.
The local pubs are very friendly and provide music every weekend. Galway City Centre is just 10 min away by car.
The B&B stands on a luxuriouly furnished property and walnut wooden floors.
The comfortable ensuite bedrooms are equipped with full size baths and power showers, hairdryers, tea/coffee making facilities, along with with 32" to 40" smart TVs and Wifi.
A varied breakfast menu includes Irish and Continental. A afternoon tea can be ordered with several home made cakes, scones and sandwiches.
There is a large dining area along with open log fires and central heating to make your stay at The Gillian Moore B&B a comfortable and memorable one.
